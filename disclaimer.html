Disclaimer

HealthConnect is an educational web application created to help users explore symptoms, compare example conditions, and learn about common medicines in a simple and user-friendly way. It is designed for learning only and should not be relied on as a substitute for professional healthcare.

Not medical advice

HealthConnect does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, or professional clinical judgement. Any conditions, medicine information, or educational guidance shown on this website are only examples based on the information stored in the prototype. They are not personalised to the user and should not be used to make medical decisions.

Educational guidance only

Any educational guidance, self-care suggestions, or home remedy examples shown in the system are provided only to support learning and awareness. They do not replace the advice of a doctor, pharmacist, nurse, or any other qualified healthcare professional. If you are worried about symptoms, medicines, or any health-related issue, you should seek professional advice.

Medicine information limitations

The medicine section is included to provide general educational information only. It does not tell users what medicine they should take and it does not check personal factors such as allergies, age, dosage, existing conditions, or interactions with other medicines. Users must always follow official packaging, pharmacist guidance, or professional medical advice before taking any medicine.

No personal or NHS data

This prototype does not collect NHS records, patient files, or sensitive personal medical data. Search information may be stored temporarily in the browser using sessionStorage or local browser storage to support features such as results pages, compare, or saved items. This data stays on the user’s device and is not intended to act as a secure medical record system.

Accuracy and completeness

Although care has been taken to structure the content clearly, HealthConnect is still a student-developed prototype and may not always be complete, current, or clinically accurate. The information shown should therefore be treated as educational sample content rather than authoritative medical information.

Emergency situations

If symptoms are severe, sudden, worsening, or life-threatening, users should seek urgent medical help immediately through the appropriate emergency services. This website must not be used in emergencies or in situations where urgent professional care is needed.
